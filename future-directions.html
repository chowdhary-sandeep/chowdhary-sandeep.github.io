Future Directions in IPCC Working Group 3

Exploring the next generation of climate mitigation research and policy integration

This page outlines the upcoming IPCC Working Group 3 report chapters and the future directions for AI-augmented literature synthesis in climate science. We focus on the critical areas of services, demand, and the enablers and barriers to effective climate mitigation.
